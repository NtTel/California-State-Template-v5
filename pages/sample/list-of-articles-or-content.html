List of articles or content

Other names: News list, report list

This is a list of articles or content that are published regularly.

Common list topics includes:

  • News releases
  • News articles
  • Blog posts
  • Publications
  • Reports

Here is a breakdown of this pattern:

  • Page title: Meaningful and concise. E.g.: News releases
  • Time period (level 2 heading): This can be by year, month and year, quarter.
  • Item: The item is listed in 3 parts
    • Title: Concise title that links to the item
    • Publication date
    • Category (optional)
  • Pagination: Optional. Use as needed.

Usage

When and how to use it

Use this pattern for related items. For instance, a News page could include news releases, news articles, and blog posts. This page would not include content that is not news related (data reports, legislative analyses).

Tip: You may want to discuss and document retention internally for these items to keep your website lean. For instance, only keep the last 3 years of new releases.

This pattern is related to the Newsroom pattern and the Article pattern . You can use all three patterns to have a cohesive news strategy for your website.

How not to use it

Do not mix unrelated items.

If possible, avoid PDFs to increase the usability and accessibility of your website. Instead, use HTML pages.
