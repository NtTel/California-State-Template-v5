Content patterns

State Template content patterns are content modules and design solutions for specific user-focused tasks. Patterns often use one or more components and explain how to adapt them to the specific context.

Blurbs

Blurbs are a great way to showcase small bits of important information

Hero Info

Hero Info is a large content section prominently placed on a web page, generally in the front and center

Figure with Captions

Figure with Captions displays a photo with a short title/description below it

News Blocks

A news blocks contains a repeating group of news items

News List

A News list is a list of news headlines with image thumbnails

News Detail

News Detail is an end page content module for news articles

Panes

Panes are separate defined areas within page that designated for displaying news, events or services

Profile Banners

A Profile Banner is a card designated for displaying state officials and representatives

Testimonials

A testimonials are used to let people know what other people think of your products or services to establish a brand trust
